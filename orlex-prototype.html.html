Description

Amendments focused on transposition of the EU Directive and improving tax transparency for companies operating in North Macedonia.

The proposed amendments to the Law on Profit Tax primarily focus on the transposition of the EU Directive. In addition, the draft law introduces further changes aimed at improving tax transparency across corporate entities.
